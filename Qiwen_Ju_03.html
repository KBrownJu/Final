In the special collection of the Fleet Library, I found some printmaking works called Interaction of Color by Josef Albers.

Through the research and imitation, I decided to integrate Albers' works into web design and add some animations, so as to facilitate the audience's interaction with the web while seeking inspiration.

Despite the simple shape and color of the web page as a whole, I attached Albers' interpretation of each work at the end of the series, which might be conducive to the audience’s in-depth understanding of the structure and colors.
