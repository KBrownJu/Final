Although JavaScript played a key role in Project 3, I was not as good at it as I am at HTML and CSS. Nevertheless, I managed to get the effect that I wanted.

Project 3 was themed on the timeline. Since I was only in Rhode Island for half a year, I had little to show but my works, so the theme was identified as my story with art and design.

The typographic design was the focus of the expression. I tried to create a horizontal page with color and typography that would automatically slide to the corresponding location when I clicked on the year.

HTF Didot was chosen as the page typeface, which is common in the titles of Harper's Bazaar and Vogue magazine. Given the letter in the project were generally of large sizes, Didot contributed to presenting a concise and modern style.
